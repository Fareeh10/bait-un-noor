About Us


Bait An Noor LiTarbiyyah (BNT) is a women-led online educational platform devoted to nurturing young minds through a meaningful integration of Islamic values and holistic learning. We are committed to building a strong foundation in faith, character, and knowledge—empowering children to grow into confident, morally grounded, and spiritually conscious individuals.

Our thoughtfully designed and visually engaging classes include Qur’an recitation with understanding, Arabic language skills, Seerah (the life and legacy of the Prophet ﷺ), and character-building rooted in timeless Islamic principles. Every session is crafted to spark curiosity, foster critical thinking, and encourage personal reflection—all within a warm, inclusive, and spiritually uplifting environment where each child is seen, supported, and inspired.
