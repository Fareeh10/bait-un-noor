About Us

Bait An Noor LiTarbiyyah (BNT) is an online initiative committed to shaping young minds through Islamic values and islamic knowledge. Our structured, visually engaging classes in Qur’an, Arabic, Seerah, and character-building are designed to foster spiritual awareness, moral clarity, and intellectual growth in every child we teach.
